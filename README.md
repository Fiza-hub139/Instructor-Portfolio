# 🎓 Instructor Portfolio

> A sleek, minimalist, and high-performance digital portfolio designed for my instrustor to showcase their academic and professional career journey.
---

## ✨ Key Experience Hubs

*   **Refined Bio & Vision:** A striking hero section featuring a professional headshot, core focus area, and instant access to your CV or Resume.
*   **Pedagogical Philosophy:** A dedicated space highlighting your core teaching values, modern methodologies, and student-first commitment.
*   **Interactive Course Catalog:** A clear, organized breakdown of current and past courses, complete with direct links to syllabi and prerequisites.
*   **Research & Publications:** A publication timeline showcasing peer-reviewed papers, books, and ongoing research projects, fully integrated with DOI deep links.
*   **Student Testimonials:** A clean, engaging section featuring feedback from peer reviews and student evaluations.
*   **Office Hours & Connect:** A modern contact bridge with real-time office availability and secure form messaging.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Structure:** HTML5
*   **Styling:** CSS3 (Custom, responsive layout)
*   **Interactivity:** JavaScript (ES6+)
